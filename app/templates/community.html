About Ntswane

Ntswane is committed to improving communication, transparency, and access to important information for the community. This platform serves as a central point for official notices, updates, and announcements.

Residents can use this website to stay informed about community developments, important service information, scheduled updates, and future communication initiatives.

The broader communication system also supports SMS notifications, email communication, and future WhatsApp integration to make sure information reaches the right people at the right time.

Our Purpose

To provide a reliable, professional, and accessible communication platform for the Ntswane community.

Our Focus
  • Clear community communication
  • Official public notices
  • Reliable updates and alerts
  • Improved resident access to information
